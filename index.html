The Covenant

Forty days. Five requirements. Every single day. Miss one and the run is dead — you start again at day one. No grace periods. No catch-up days. This is the wilderness.

  1. PRAY — 20 minutes of prayer, minimum.
  2. READ — one chapter of Scripture, minimum.
  3. FAST — keep the fast you choose below, all 40 days.
  4. TRAIN — 45 minutes of physical training. The body is a temple.
  5. ABSTAIN — zero vices. You know what yours are.
